Progress

Week 1

Get to know your dog even better.

Welcome to your Good Dog Program. Dogs are amazing! They can also be stressful. Over the next 6 weeks you will foster a new relationship with your dog. Dogs thrive with structure and routine. Dogs who act out or misbehave simply just don't know better. There is a lot to learn on your journey - for you and your pup. This week you will lean the basics of How Dogs Think and the two most essential commands, Sit and Come. You'll be amazed at how much progress can be made in one week. We KNOW you love your dog, you wouldn't be here if you didn't. That being said, we promise you that after the completion of this program you will know your dog even better and you will have a deeper relationship with them.
